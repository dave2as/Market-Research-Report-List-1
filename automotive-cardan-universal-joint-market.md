<p><p>The Automotive Cardan Universal Joint market is segmented into Single Cardan, Double Cardan, and Others. Single Cardan joints consist of one yoke connected by a cross-shaped pin, facilitating basic angular movement. Double Cardan joints feature two yokes, allowing for greater versatility and reduced vibration at higher angles. The "Others" category encompasses specialized designs and variations, such as constant velocity joints, which cater to specific vehicle requirements, enhancing performance and stability in diverse driving conditions.</p></p>
