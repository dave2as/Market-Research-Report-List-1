<p><p>The amateur football helmet market is primarily divided into two types based on materials: ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) and TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane). ABS helmets are favored for their lightweight and impact-resistant properties, making them affordable and widely used among recreational players. In contrast, TPU helmets offer enhanced cushioning and flexibility, providing superior shock absorption and comfort, which attracts both serious amateurs and youth players. Each material caters to different performance and budget needs in football safety gear.</p></p>
