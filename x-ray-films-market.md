<p><p>The X-Ray films market is categorized into screen type films, non-screen type films, and others. Screen type films are designed to be used in conjunction with intensifying screens, which enhance the image quality and reduce radiation exposure. Non-screen type films, on the other hand, operate independently without screens, requiring higher radiation doses for imaging. The 'others' category encompasses specialized films, such as digital or flexible films, used in niche applications or advanced imaging technologies. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes in medical settings.</p></p>
