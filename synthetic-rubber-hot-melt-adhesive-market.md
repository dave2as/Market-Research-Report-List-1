<p><ul><li>HTV</li><li>RTV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic rubber hot melt adhesives are categorized into two main types: HTV (Heat Transfer Vulcanization) and RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanization). HTV adhesives require heat to cure and bond materials, offering strong adhesion and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance applications. RTV adhesives, on the other hand, cure at room temperature, providing ease of use and quick bonding times, suitable for various consumer and industrial applications. Both types contribute to the versatility and effectiveness of synthetic rubber adhesives in diverse markets.</p></p>
