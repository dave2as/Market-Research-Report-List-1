<p><p>The replacement tires market is divided primarily into radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature a radial ply construction, offering enhanced stability, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life, making them popular in passenger vehicles and light trucks. In contrast, bias tires have plies arranged at diagonal angles, providing a stiffer sidewall and greater load capacity, often preferred for heavy-duty applications like agricultural and construction vehicles. Each type serves distinct needs, influencing consumer choices based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
