<p><ul><li>Eliminate Bubbles</li><li>Suppress Bubbles</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defoamer for injection molding market is categorized into three main types: eliminate bubbles, suppress bubbles, and others. "Eliminate bubbles" focuses on completely removing air trapped in the material, preventing defects in the final product. "Suppress bubbles" involves reducing bubble formation during the molding process, ensuring a smoother finish without completely eliminating air. "Others" encompasses various specialized formulations that may offer additional functionalities or cater to specific materials and processing conditions, enhancing overall mold quality and performance.</p></p>
