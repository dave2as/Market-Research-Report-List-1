<p><p>The Cable Granulator market comprises two main types: dry type and wet type copper cable granulators. Dry type granulators utilize mechanical processes to separate copper from insulation without using water, making them energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. In contrast, wet type granulators employ water during the separation process, enhancing recovery rates by preventing dust and air pollution. Both types cater to different operation scales and efficiency needs, influencing their adoption across various recycling facilities and industries.</p></p>
