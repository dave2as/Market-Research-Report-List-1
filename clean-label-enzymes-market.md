<p><p>The Clean Label Enzymes market encompasses various enzyme types, including carbohydrases, amylases, cellulases, proteases, and others. Carbohydrases facilitate the breakdown of carbohydrates, enhancing food textures. Amylases specifically target starches, improving digestibility and sugar release. Cellulases break down cellulose, aiding in fiber processing and improving digestibility in products. Proteases assist in protein degradation, enhancing flavor and texture in foods. Other enzymes may include lipases and lactases, contributing to diverse applications in food and beverage industries, promoting cleaner ingredient labels.</p></p>
